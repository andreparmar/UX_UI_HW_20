Interview Plan

For this study, I conducted 5 user interviews based on forms of travel and if they rely on other customer reviews as well provide them. I went for a broader approach as well to see if there were also other ways of getting our app out to all sorts of users. After completing the interviews, I noticed in the data that many people rely on real customer reviews; but don't provide them so much. Ages varied throughout the study, but mostly stayed in the 20-40 years of age range.
